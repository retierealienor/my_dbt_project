This model provides a summary of user activity, focusing on their order history and purchasing behavior. It aggregates key metrics for each user, such as:

- **ca_global**: Total CA generated.
- **panier_moyen**: Average basket per customer.
- **nb_commandes**: Total of orders
- **nb_moyen_produits_par_panier**: Average product per basket.
- **prix_moyen_produit**: Average proce per product.
